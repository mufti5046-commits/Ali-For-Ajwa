A Letter for Ajwa

♡

Tap the envelope to open it.

Dear Ajwa,

I hope your days are always filled with happiness, gentle moments, and beautiful reasons to smile.

May the sky watch over you when the world feels heavy, and may the earth keep you safe wherever your footsteps lead. May every cloud above you bring comfort, and every flower you pass remind you that softness can still survive in a difficult world.

Please keep your beauty, not only the beauty seen by the eyes, but also the kindness, warmth, and quiet strength that live within you. And please do not forget to smile. Your smile may be small to you, but it can make an ordinary day feel brighter.

Whenever you feel tired, remember that you do not have to be strong every second. It is all right to rest. It is all right to take your time. Even the sky needs clouds before it can show a rainbow.

I hope life brings you people who understand your silence, moments that heal your heart, and dreams that slowly find their way to you. May you always be surrounded by sincere love, peaceful thoughts, and the courage to continue becoming the person you wish to be.

No matter how uncertain tomorrow may seem, I hope you always remember that you are precious, worthy, and deserving of every good thing that comes your way.

Take care of yourself, Ajwa. Keep shining gently, keep blooming in your own way, and never lose the beautiful light that makes you who you are.

With warm wishes,
Ali

Take Care, Ajwa
